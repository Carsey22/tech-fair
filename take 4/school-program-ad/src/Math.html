Mathematics Applications and Interpretations

Overview

The Mathematics IB course is applications and interpretations. This course covers various broad units of math across: Algebra, Geometry, Statistics, and Calculus. This class discusses how to interpret different forms of mathematics that incorporate different areas of math and furthermore how to apply these types of math to solve complex equations.

Teacher: Mrs. Tiffany Jenkins

Math IA

One major part of the IB Math course is the math internal assessment. This assessment is a written paper investigating a topic in which you must incorporate mathematics from an area of math involved such as Algebra or Geometry. This paper involves months-long development with entire workdays dedicated to just finding a topic as well as finding data and doing your math. This paper is graded on an IB scale from 1-20 and has the chance of being specifically reviewed by IB itself.
